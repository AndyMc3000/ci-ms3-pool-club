One Passion. One Family.

Welcome to the Cill na Martra Pool Club website. Our Pool Club was setup in 2012, when a group of 6 pool players began meeting for an evening of pool and banter on Friday evenings. Since then our membership has grown to over 30 players, who now play in 2 pool leagues each year. We run a Summer league and a Winter league. Club members enjoy being part of a Club where they can meet new people, improve their pool skills, all while enjoying a healthy dose of fun. Players of all shapes, sizes, and abililities are invited to join. You will be met with a warm welcome, and if you are a beginner you will find plenty of help and advice. Don't be shy, sign up today!
