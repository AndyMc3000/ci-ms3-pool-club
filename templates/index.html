One Passion. One Family.

Welcome to the Cill na Martra Pool Club website. Our Pool Club was setup in 2012, when a group of 6 pool players began meeting for an evening of pool and banter on Friday evenings at O'Murchu's Bar in Cill na Martra. Since then our membership has grown to over 30 players, who now play in 2 pool leagues each year. We run a Summer league and a Winter league. Club members enjoy being part of a Club where they can meet new people, improve their pool skills, all while enjoying a healthy dose of fun. Players of all shapes, sizes, and abililities are invited to join. You will be met with a warm welcome, and if you are a beginner you will find plenty of help and advice. Don't be shy, sign up today!

Why Join?

Pool is as much a social sport as competitive one. Members have a place where they can meet friends and make new ones. In addition Pool is healthy and benefical sport. You will burn calories and buidl musle tone as you are walking, bending, and strtching at the table. It improves critical thinking, builds your ability to focus, and sharpens your mind. Once a new rack is broken, there an infinite number of possibilities for how best to play your game. The best pool players are the ones who make wise shot decisions, as opposed to those with great cueing skills.

And at our Club we do it all with a healthy doe of good craic. Feel free to come along on a Friday night to see for yourself how enjoyable a few frames of Pool can be.

League Tables

Once you sign up and join our Club, you will automatically be entered into our Pool Leagues. Leagues run twice a year. Each player must play a match against every other player twice. A player wins a match once they win 3 games (or the best of 5 games). Points are given for winning zero games (1 point), winning 1 game (2 points), winning 2 games (3 points), and winning 3 games (4 points).
