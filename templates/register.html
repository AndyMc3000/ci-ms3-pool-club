Why join?


Pool is an indoor sport which can be played competitively or socially. Snooker, billiards & pool are skilful games that can be played by anyone, regardless of age, gender or ability. Cue sports provide a fun and sociable way to stay active, improve your concentration and increase your strength and muscle control. Once you've mastered the skills, you could enter local league matches and amateur level competitions across the country. Cue sports are very inclusive as the rules and equipment are the same for able-bodied and disabled players.
